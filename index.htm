Alan Turing

Founder of Computer Science, mathematician, code breaker, and philosopher

  • Born on June 23, 1912 in Maida Vale, London, England
  • Attended King's College 1931 - 1934 for Mathmatics
  • Fellow of King's College from 1932 - 1935 in Quantum Mechanics, probability, and logic
  • Attended Princeton from 1936 - 1938 earning a Ph.D.
  • Was introduced to the German Enigma cipher machine during his time back at Cambridge from 1938 - 1939
  • In 1946 Computer and Software Design Leading the world was published
  • In 1950 published The Turing Test for Machine Intelligence
  • Died june 7, 1954 in Wilmolow, Cheshire, England
